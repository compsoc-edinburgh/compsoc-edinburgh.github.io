FAQ

What is Hack the Burgh?

StudentHack is a hackathon where students passionate about programming spend an entire weekend building awesome projects. Entrepreneurs, designers and developers collaborate to build hacks & apps - for web, mobile & hardware - in teams up to 4.

How much does it cost?

StudentHack is completely free to attend. However, tickets are extremely limited and are only available on a first-come-first-serve basis.
